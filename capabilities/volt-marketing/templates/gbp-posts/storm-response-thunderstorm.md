**Template ID:** storm-response-thunderstorm

**When to use:** During or immediately after a severe thunderstorm with known outages or damage

---

Colorado Springs is getting hit hard right now, and we know many of you are dealing with the aftermath — downed lines, outages, and damage you can see (and damage you can't).

**If you see a downed power line:** Stay far away and call 911 immediately. Assume every downed line is live.

Phoenix Electric is here for storm-related electrical emergencies. If you're experiencing any of the following, give us a call:

- Power is back on but breakers won't reset
- You see scorch marks, smell burning, or notice sparking at your panel
- Outdoor fixtures, mast, or weatherhead are damaged
- Your generator ran during the outage but is showing error codes
- Lights are flickering or outlets aren't working even though the neighborhood has power

Storm damage to your electrical system isn't always obvious. Surges, water intrusion, and physical damage to service lines can create hazards that show up days or weeks later.

We're prioritizing safety calls and working through requests as fast as we can. Please be patient with response times — we're a local crew and we take care of our community.
