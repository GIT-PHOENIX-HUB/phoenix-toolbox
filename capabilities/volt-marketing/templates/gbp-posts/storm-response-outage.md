Another outage across Colorado Springs. Whether it lasted two hours or two days, that uneasy feeling of being without power is hard to shake.

**If you have a generator:**
Now is a good time for a post-event check. After an extended run, verify that your unit returned to "auto" standby mode and note any error codes on the display. If your generator ran for more than a few hours, consider scheduling a service visit — extended runs can accelerate your maintenance timeline.

If anything seemed off during the outage — delayed start, unusual sounds, error codes, or a failure to start — call us. Better to catch a small issue now than discover it during the next outage.

**If you don't have a generator:**
You just experienced exactly why thousands of Colorado Springs homeowners have made the switch to standby backup power. A Generac whole-home generator would have kept everything running — lights, kitchen, furnace, internet, home office — automatically, without you lifting a finger.
