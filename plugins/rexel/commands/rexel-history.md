# Rexel Purchase History

View top purchased items and spend analysis from Rexel invoices.

## Instructions

Use `rexel_top_items` to show rankings. Ask the user or infer:
- **Sort by:** spend (default), quantity, or frequency
- **Category filter:** Wire & Cable, Lighting, Breakers, etc.
- **Limit:** How many items (default 20)

Also use `rexel_category_summary` to show the full spend breakdown by category.

Present insights alongside the data:
- "Wire & Cable is 35% of total Rexel spend"
- "Your most purchased item is 14-2 Romex with 212 purchases"
- "Top 10 items account for X% of total spend"
